This section will cover:

- Authentication strategies — `JWTStrategy` (the default) and `BasicStrategy`, or writing your own
- Requiring auth per-route with `@Auth`
- Role-based access control with `@RequiresRole`
- Resource-level access control lists with `@Protect` and the `AccessControlList` model
