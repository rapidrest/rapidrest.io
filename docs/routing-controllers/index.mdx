# Routing & Controllers

Routes in RapidREST are composed of plain classes decorated with `@Route` and one or more methods that have been decoratored with at least one desired
HTTP-method (e.g. `@Get`, `@Post`, `@Put`). There's no separate router file. A route class is discovered and registered the moment it's exported
anywhere under `src/` (see [Auto-Discovery](/docs/core-concepts/auto-discovery)).

- **[Defining Routes](/docs/routing-controllers/defining-routes)** - `@Route`, the HTTP method decorators, and how handlers get called.
- **[Request Parameters](/docs/routing-controllers/request-parameters)** - pulling data out of the request with `@Param`, `@Query`, `@Header`, `@Body`, and friends.
- **[Lifecycle & Validation](/docs/routing-controllers/lifecycle-validation)** - `@Before`/`@After` middleware chains, `@Validate`, and `@ContentType`.
